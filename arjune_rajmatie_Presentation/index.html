Why AniJS?

AniJS is a Declarative handling library for Animations

aniJS

Benefits of AniJS

  1. Animation could be written by declarative commands right into HTML document.

  2. Animation could be easily managed with declarations that express logic.

syntax example

Understanding AniJS

AniJS brings uniqueness to web design by providing:

  • Interactivity
  • Vibrancy
  • Flexibility, and
  • Energy

Sentence and Definitions

Each part of aniJS sentence calls a definition.

Basic definitions:

  • If
  • On
  • Do
  • To

Example

If mouseover, On.item, Do $addClass active To .item.active

Sentence and Definitions

Extended definitions:

  • Before
  • After
  • Helper

data-anijs="if: click, do: bounceIn, before: beforeAnimationFunctionName" 
data-anijs="if: click, do: bounceIn, after: afterAnimationFunctionName"
data-anijs="if: click, do: bounceIn, after: afterAnimationFunctionName, helper: animationHelperInstanceName"
